# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Hybrid Club serves people training for hybrid performance: strength and running in the same weekly routine. They open the app during training or planning moments, often on mobile, to see the day's session, log sets or cardio, track hydration and nutrition, and understand progress without stopping their workout flow.

## Product Purpose

Hybrid Club exists to turn a hybrid training plan into a clear daily operating system. Success means the athlete can quickly identify today's workout, execute strength and cardio sessions, record useful data, and keep consistency over time through history, progress signals, and plan adjustments.

## Brand Personality

Premium, profissional, clean. The product should feel disciplined and trustworthy, with a polished mobile PWA experience that supports focus during training instead of competing for attention.

## Anti-references

Do not make the product look like a generic fitness app, a screen full of identical cards, or an amateur visual experiment. Avoid generic gym-app energy, repetitive card grids, inconsistent components, noisy decoration, and visual choices that make the app feel less serious than the athlete using it.

## Design Principles

Lead with the workout of the day: the first screen should make the next training action obvious.

Support fast logging: kg, reps, min, km, sets, and targets must stay easy to scan and enter while the user is in motion.

Keep analytics in service of action: progress, history, hydration, and nutrition should clarify decisions rather than dominate the interface.

Preserve premium restraint: use contrast, spacing, hierarchy, and consistent component behavior before adding decoration.

Honor the hybrid identity: strength and running are both first-class parts of the product language and information architecture.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and core controls. Preserve reduced-motion alternatives for animated effects, keep tap targets comfortable on mobile, and ensure the app remains usable if WebGL or visual effects fail.
